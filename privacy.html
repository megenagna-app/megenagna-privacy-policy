Privacy Policy

Last Updated:

This policy explains how Megenag Dating App ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information. By using our services, you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Basic details: Name, email, phone number, date of birth
  • Profile information: Gender, sexual orientation, relationship preferences
  • User content: Profile photos, bio, messages to other users

1.2 Technical Information

  • Device data: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
  • Usage data: Features used, time spent, interactions with other users
  • Location data: Precise GPS location (when enabled)

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Core Services

  • Create and maintain your dating profile
  • Show potential matches based on your preferences and location
  • Enable messaging between matched users

2.2 Service Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve our algorithms
  • Develop new features based on user behavior
  • Monitor and prevent fraudulent activity

2.3 Communications

  • Send important service notifications
  • Provide customer support
  • Send promotional offers (you can opt-out)

3. Data Sharing

3.1 With Other Users

Your profile information (name, photos, bio) is visible to other users based on your privacy settings.

3.2 With Service Providers

  • Cloud services: AWS for data storage
  • Analytics: Firebase for usage statistics
  • Payments: Stripe for subscription processing

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law or to protect our rights.

4. Data Security

  • End-to-end encryption for all messages
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Limited employee access to user data
  • Two-factor authentication for staff accounts

5. Your Rights

5.1 Access and Control

You can:

  • View and update your profile information in the app
  • Download your data through account settings
  • Delete your account permanently

5.2 Communication Preferences

Manage notification settings in your device or app settings.

5.3 Location Data

Control location sharing through your device permissions.

6. Data Retention

  • Active accounts: Data retained until deletion
  • Inactive accounts: Deleted after 24 months of inactivity
  • Messages: Deleted when either user deletes their account

7. International Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your residence. We use standard contractual clauses for international data transfers.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Policy Updates

We will notify users of significant changes through the app or email. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
