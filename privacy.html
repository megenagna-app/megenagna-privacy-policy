🔒 Your Privacy Matters

This policy explains how Megenagna Dating App ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information. By using our services, you agree to this policy.

Data Deletion URL: This page

1Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Basic details: Name, email, phone number, date of birth
  • Profile information: Gender, sexual orientation, relationship preferences
  • User content: Profile photos, bio, messages to other users
  • Preferences: Match criteria, notification settings, app customization

1.2 Technical Information

  • Device data: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
  • Usage data: Features used, time spent, interactions with other users
  • Location data: Precise GPS location (when enabled, for matching purposes)
  • Network information: IP address, connection type, carrier information

2How We Use Your Information

2.1 Core Services

  • Create and maintain your dating profile
  • Show potential matches based on your preferences and location
  • Enable messaging between matched users
  • Provide personalized dating recommendations

2.2 Service Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve our matching algorithms
  • Develop new features based on user behavior and feedback
  • Monitor and prevent fraudulent activity and fake profiles
  • Conduct research to enhance user experience

2.3 Communications

  • Send important service notifications and updates
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries
  • Send promotional offers and dating tips (you can opt-out anytime)
  • Notify you of new matches and messages

3Data Sharing

3.1 With Other Users

Your profile information (name, photos, bio, interests) is visible to other users based on your privacy settings and matching criteria.

3.2 With Service Providers

  • Cloud services: AWS for secure data storage and processing
  • Analytics: Firebase for anonymous usage statistics and crash reporting
  • Payments: Stripe for secure subscription processing
  • Communication: Twilio for SMS verification and notifications

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law, to protect our rights, or in emergency situations where user safety is at risk.

4Data Security

  • End-to-end encryption for all private messages and sensitive data
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing by third-party experts
  • Limited employee access to user data with strict confidentiality agreements
  • Two-factor authentication for all staff accounts and sensitive operations
  • Automatic security updates and vulnerability patching

5Your Rights

5.1 Access and Control

You can:

  • View and update your profile information in the app settings
  • Download your complete data package through account settings
  • Delete your account permanently with immediate effect
  • Facebook-Specific Deletion: Users who registered via Facebook may request data deletion per Section 11

5.2 Communication Preferences

Manage all notification settings in your device or app preferences. You have full control over:

  • Push notifications for new matches and messages
  • Email marketing communications
  • SMS alerts and reminders

5.3 Location Data

Control location sharing through your device permissions. You can:

  • Enable/disable location services anytime
  • Set precise or approximate location sharing
  • Use the app without location services (with limited matching features)

6Data Retention

  • Active accounts: Data retained until account deletion
  • Inactive accounts: Deleted after 24 months of inactivity
  • Messages: Deleted when either user deletes their account
  • Photos: Removed from servers immediately after deletion
  • Facebook Data Deletion Requests: Fulfilled within 30 days regardless of account status

7International Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your residence. We ensure protection through:

  • Standard contractual clauses for international data transfers
  • Adequacy decisions where applicable
  • Same level of protection as required by your local laws

8Children's Privacy

Our services are strictly for users 18 years and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children and immediately delete any accounts found to be created by minors.

9Policy Updates

We will notify users of significant changes through:

  • In-app notifications and banners
  • Email announcements for major updates
  • Updated "Last Updated" date on this page

Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11Facebook Data Deletion Instructions

If you registered using Facebook Login and wish to delete your data:

How to request deletion:

  • Email us at privacy@megenagna.com with subject: "Facebook Data Deletion Request"
  • Include your Facebook ID (found in your Facebook profile URL)
  • Example Facebook ID format:
    https://www.facebook.com/profile.php?id=YOUR_FACEBOOK_ID
  • Include the email address associated with your account

What we delete:

  • All personal data collected via Facebook Login
  • Profile information and preferences
  • Usage data linked to your Facebook ID
  • Connection history and match data
  • Message history and media files

Processing timeline:

  • Requests processed within 30 days
  • Confirmation email sent upon completion
  • Backup data permanently erased within 90 days
  • No recovery possible after deletion

Alternative methods:

  • Delete your entire account in-app:
    Profile → Settings → Account → Delete Account
  • Use the automatic deletion tool in our help center
