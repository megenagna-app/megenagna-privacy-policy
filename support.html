Getting Started

1. How do I create an account?
Download the app from the App Store or Google Play Store, open it, and follow the on-screen instructions to sign up with your email or social media accounts.
2. How do I set up my profile?
After signing up, go to your profile section to add photos, write a bio, and set your preferences. A complete profile increases your matches!
3. How do I find matches?
Use the discovery feature to browse profiles. Swipe right to like someone or left to pass. You'll be notified when there's a mutual match.

Account Management

1. How do I reset my password?
Go to the login screen, tap "Forgot Password," enter your email, and follow the instructions sent to your inbox.
2. How do I update my email address?
Go to Settings ‚Üí Account ‚Üí Email and follow the verification process to update your email address.
3. How do I delete my account?
Go to Settings ‚Üí Account ‚Üí Delete Account. Please note this action is permanent and cannot be undone.

Safety & Reporting

1. How do I report a user?
Go to the user's profile, tap the three-dot menu, select "Report," and choose the reason for your report.
2. How do I block someone?
Visit the user's profile, tap the three-dot menu, and select "Block User." Blocked users cannot contact you.
3. What should I do if I feel unsafe?
Immediately stop communication, block the user, and report them to our safety team. Your safety is our priority.

Subscription & Payments

1. How do subscriptions work?
We offer various subscription plans (Daily, Weekly, Monthly, Quarterly) that provide premium features. Subscriptions automatically renew unless canceled.
2. How do I cancel my subscription?
Go to Settings ‚Üí Subscriptions ‚Üí Manage Subscription. Cancellation must be done at least 24 hours before the renewal date.
3. How do I request a refund?
Refund requests must be submitted through the app store where you made the purchase (Apple App Store or Google Play Store).

Technical Support

1. The app is crashing/not loading
- Ensure you have the latest app version
- Restart your device
- Clear app cache/data
- Reinstall the app
2. Notifications aren't working
- Check app notification settings
- Verify device notification permissions
- Ensure you're logged in
3. Photos won't upload
- Check your internet connection
- Verify photo format (JPEG, PNG)
- Ensure photo size is under 10MB
